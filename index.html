Parul University

Parul University is a private university in Vadodara, Gujarat, India

It was established by the Parul Group of Institutions (PGI) on 24th February 2009 and started its academic activities from August 2 2009.Parul University is one of the premier educational institutions in Vadodara, India. It is one of the largest.

Established in 1965 by the Parul Educ Group, Parul University is one of the premier educational institutions in Vadodara, India. It is one of the largest, premier, and most popular universities in the country.


About Us

Parul University offers undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and PhD programs in fields such as engineering, architecture, and commerce. It also has an auditorium, badminton court, basketball court, cafeteria, classrooms, cricket ground, digital library, and hospital. Parul University is accredited by an A+ Grade by NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council), NABH, Central Council for Homeopathy, Bar Council of India, and the National Medical Commission. In 2023, NIRF Innovation Rankings ranked it among the top 50 universities in India. It has also been ranked among the “Top 50” colleges in India BCA by India Today. Some say Parul University has the best campus in Gujarat, with excellent teaching and well-maintained labs

Placements

Parul University, Vadodara, Gujarat, has a placement track record of placing over 24,000 students in around 2,200 companies all over India. The university has over 2,000 recruiting companies and more than 2,500 annual student placements. In 2023, Parul University's highest package was recorded as INR 30.00 LPA. The average salary offered is INR 7.02 Lacs per annum. Parul University's training and placement cell regularly arranges mock interviews, coordinates between companies and students, and organizes training programs.

Transport

Parul University has a fleet of 63 buses and 20 passenger vehicles. The university provides transport services to and from campus year-round. The buses run daily, transporting over 10,000 students and 1,000 faculty members. The buses also run daily from nearby cities such as Ahmedabad, Anand, Nadiad, Karjan, and Bharuch. The buses pick up and drop off students from over 100 bus stops across and around the city. The buses are driven by experienced drivers in proper uniforms.

History

A multidisciplinary destination of learning and innovation, propelling quality in higher education with a record of being India’s youngest private university to receive NAAC A++ accreditation in the first cycle. Situated in Vadodara, Gujarat, Parul University, is an embodiment of the nation's essence of cultural heritage blended with modern innovations and academic practices for student enrichment, while fostering national and global development. The University is an amalgamation of faculties and institutes that offer a plethora of diploma, undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in numerous disciplines. Through its uniquely structured, industry linked and field aligned programs, the University holds a noteworthy record of fulfilling the infinite dreams of students, by launching their lucrative careers towards high trajectories through start-up incubation and impeccable placement records. The 125-acre eco-friendly campus is home to over 43,000 students from every State of India and over 2,500 international students from 68 countries, making Parul University a truly culturally global destination. In addition to its NAAC A++ accreditation, the University holds global memberships in bodies such as the Association of Commonwealth Universities. The University’s stamps of quality extend to its DISR recognition for quality research, NABL accreditation for quality in clinical medical research, NABH accreditation for quality healthcare and ARIIA Top 50 ranking for innovation achievements nationwide. In recognition of Parul University’s excellence in education it has been awarded for being the Best Private University in Western India by Praxis Media and Best University in Placements by ASSOCHAM and the Most Outstanding University in West Zone for having Highest Nationalities in Campus at the World Education Summit Awards.

Can I get scholarship in Parul University?

We have on offer three categories of scholarships, cultural scholarships, sports scholarships and defence scholarships. The students who satisfy the basic requirements of these scholarships, with the necessary ethical academic standards and good conduct, can be granted this financial aid.

Can I get direct admission in Parul University?

Candidates who fulfil the minimum eligibility criteria for this course get direct admission to MBA. To study MBA at Parul University, candidates must be graduated with 50% aggregate in any stream to be eligible for this course. Admission to MBA at Parul University is completely merit-based.

Is Parul good for placements?

It offers good placements every year for BTech course, especially the CSE branch. The complete Parul University BTech placement details are yet to be released for the batch passing out in 2023. However, as per the media reports, in 2023, the highest package offered to BTech CSE students stood at INR 9 LPA.

Does Parul University have gym facilities?

The university has on-campus amenities such as gymnasium, swimming pool, open-air theatre, banking service and hospital are also available to the students. Separate hostel blocks for NRIs and International students equipped with air conditioner and other modern facilities are also available.
